About Our Mission

Cultivating Innovation in Agriculture

Bempet Multimerchandise LTD (Bempet Organic Farm) is a company headquartered in Lagos, Nigeria, that focuses on sustainable ways to enhance food security using the black soldier fly. For over a decade, Bempet Multimerchandise LTD has been at the forefront of agricultural innovation. Black Soldier Fly Revolution

Our primary goal is to develop commercial black soldier fly larvae facilities for farmers and mentor small-scale farmers on how they, too, can start producing black soldier fly larvae for alternative animal feed and crop fertilizer.

10+ Years of Practical Industry Experience

We have built a global community of farmers and helped those who have shut down their farms due to the skyrocketing cost of conventional animal feed and crop fertilizer return to farming, adopting a natural means of sustainably feeding their animals, and in the same process, also producing crop fertilizers for their crops and gardens.

With this eco-friendly method of producing sustainable animal feeds and crop fertilizer, we are focusing on those in fisheries/Aquaculture, livestock farming, crop production, and the Agricultural sector generally.
